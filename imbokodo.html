Unleashing Her Power

Empowering women in KZN to become leaders in technology

Our Vision

To be the leading tech hub empowering women in KZN by providing them with the resources, mentorship, and opportunities to become innovators, entrepreneurs, and leaders in the tech industry.

Our Mission

KZN Tech Mbokodo is dedicated to creating a vibrant ecosystem where women in technology can thrive. We aim to nurture the next generation of female tech leaders by offering a supportive community, access to resources, and opportunities for personal and professional growth. Through our platform, we're committed to removing barriers, promoting diversity, and inspiring women to step into their full potential in the ever-evolving tech world.

Why Join KZN Tech Mbokodo?

Empowerment and Support

KZN Tech Mbokodo provides a safe, empowering space for women to grow, collaborate, and succeed in tech. It's a place where your voice is heard, and your ambitions are supported.

Mentorship and Networking

By joining, women gain access to experienced mentors, industry leaders, and a network of like-minded individuals, offering guidance, collaboration, and inspiration.

Opportunities for Growth

Whether you're looking to start your own tech venture, innovate within an existing organization, or learn new skills, KZN Tech Mbokodo offers a platform with a wealth of opportunities tailored for women.

Breaking Barriers

We are committed to reducing the gender gap in tech and helping women take their rightful place at the forefront of innovation.

Inclusive Community

The hub fosters a diverse and inclusive environment where women from all backgrounds and experiences can connect, collaborate, and build tech-driven solutions for the future.
