A Message from Our Founder

My name is Sphiwe Khuzwayo, an IT Technician, Software Developer. Growing up in KwaMashu and Newlands West, I faced significant challenges due to the lack of opportunities to learn about technology, especially while attending government schools. Determined to make a difference, I pursued a career in IT with the goal of returning to my community to bring about positive change and uplift others by enhancing their understanding of the technology space.

One vivid memory that drives my passion is from my high school days. I remember struggling to complete a computer assignment because our school had only one outdated computer for the entire class. This experience fueled my determination to ensure that future generations wouldn't face the same obstacles.

I have founded several companies and non-profit organizations to assist those in need, primarily focusing on learning exchange sessions to support underserved communities with knowledge of technology and artificial intelligence.

Tech Horizon is a dynamic community initiative dedicated to establishing KwaZulu-Natal (KZN) as a vibrant and essential tech hub. With a significant population of tech professionals and many aspiring to join the industry, KZN is poised for growth. However, there is a disconnect between these groups, as local tech professionals are often unknown to those aspiring to enter the field. Tech Horizon aims to bridge this gap by fostering connections, creating networking opportunities, and opening new doors.
